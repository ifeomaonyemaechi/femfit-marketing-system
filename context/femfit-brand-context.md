# FemFit.fit — Brand Context File
# Last updated: April 2026
# Used by all agents in the FemFit.fit marketing system

## BRAND OVERVIEW
Name: FemFit.fit
Type: Women's fitness DTC brand
Products: Gym wear, shoes, home workout equipment
Founded by: A woman who trains, for women who actually train
Platform: Shopify (ecommerce), Klaviyo (email), Instagram, TikTok
Markets: Nigeria, South Africa, UK and US (primarily English-speaking)
Price point: Mid-market — quality-conscious, value-aware

## MISSION
To make gear that works as hard as the women who wear it.
No fluff. No overpromising. Just functional kit that holds up.

## TARGET CUSTOMER PROFILE

### Demographics
- Women aged 24-42
- Mix of stay-at-home mums, entrepreneurs, working professionals
- Disposable income for quality fitness products
- Value-conscious — wants to know money is well spent

### Psychographics
- Self-motivated but time-poor
- Training at home, at the gym, or both
- Values efficiency — wants gear that works without thinking about it
- Skeptical of influencer culture and overhyped brands
- Body-positive but battling real-life pressures

### What She Values
- Quality over quantity
- Fast delivery and hassle-free returns
- Brands that treat her like an adult
- Products that fit real bodies
- Feeling supported without being patronised

### What Frustrates Her
- Gym wear that looks good online but fits terribly
- Equipment marketed as premium that breaks in 3 months
- Brands that use body-positive language but show one body type
- Slow shipping and poor customer service
- Vague sizing guides

### What She Wants to Feel
- Confident in her workout gear
- Motivated to move even on hard days
- Good about investing in herself
- Understood by the brand she buys from

## BRAND VOICE

### Personality Pillars
1. MOTIVATED — Shows up ready. Action-oriented. Assumes she's in.
2. NO-NONSENSE — Cuts the fluff. States benefits plainly.
3. EMPOWERING — Lifts women up without being performative.
4. QUALITY-OBSESSED — Specific about materials and construction.
5. RELATABLE — Real life, not highlight reels.

### BANNED WORDS — NEVER USE
amazing, incredible, game-changing, transform your body, slay,
queen, goddess, boss babe, exclusive collection, luxurious,
best-selling, perfect for any occasion, unleash, empower your
journey, elevate, curated, effortless, seamless, revolutionise,
supercharge

### WORDS TO USE
squat-proof, built for, real women, holds up, train, no-fuss,
fast delivery, your pace, gear, fits right, functional, durable,
double-stitched, moisture-wicking, compression, squat test

### Voice Test
Before publishing anything, ask:
"Would a woman who trains say this to another woman who trains?"
If it sounds like marketing copy, rewrite it.
