# Skill: Generate Content Calendar
# FemFit.fit Marketing Operations System

## WHAT THIS SKILL DOES
Generates a structured content calendar for FemFit.fit
across all active platforms. Called by the content-strategist
agent. Produces a day-by-day plan with platform, pillar,
format, and concept for each piece — ready to execute
without further planning.

## WHEN TO USE THIS SKILL
Use when:
- A monthly content calendar is needed
- A weekly content pack needs a schedule
- A campaign needs a content rollout plan
- The content-strategist agent is planning ahead

## CALENDAR TYPES

### Monthly Calendar (30 days)
Platforms: Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, Pinterest
Output: Day-by-day table for the full month
Pieces: 40-50 total across all platforms
Use for: Monthly planning sessions

### Weekly Content Pack (7 days)
Platforms: Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, Pinterest
Output: Day-by-day table for one week
Pieces: 8-12 total
Use for: Weekly batch creation sessions

### Campaign Calendar
Platforms: All relevant to the campaign
Output: Campaign timeline with content mapped to phases
Pieces: Varies by campaign scope
Use for: Product launches, seasonal pushes, sales events

## CONTENT RATIO RULES — ENFORCE THESE

### Instagram
- 40% Reels
- 30% Carousels
- 20% Feed posts
- 10% Stories (daily)
- Product content: maximum 30% of all posts
- Posting frequency: 3x feed per week, daily Stories

### TikTok
- 60% talking head
- 25% training footage
- 15% list format
- Posting frequency: 4-5x per week

### Facebook
- Repurposed from Instagram with slight rewrites
- Community questions and polls
- Posting frequency: 3-4x per week

### Pinterest
- Product pins, workout guides, lifestyle imagery
- Every pin links to Shopify or blog
- Posting frequency: 5-7 pins per week

## PILLAR DISTRIBUTION
Across any 4-week period, distribute evenly:
- Real Training, Real Results: 25% of content
- Fit and Function: 25% of content
- Home Gym Life: 25% of content
- Train For You: 25% of content

## WEEKLY RHYTHM — DEFAULT SCHEDULE
Monday: Instagram carousel (Fit and Function)
Tuesday: TikTok talking head (Real Training)
Wednesday: Instagram feed post (Train For You)
Thursday: Instagram Reel (Home Gym Life)
Friday: TikTok list format (Fit and Function)
Saturday: Pinterest batch (5-7 pins)
Sunday: Facebook community post (any pillar)

## MONTHLY THEME STRUCTURE
Week 1: Foundation — introduce the month's focus
Week 2: Product — spotlight one product in depth
Week 3: Community — customer stories and UGC
Week 4: Momentum — drive action before month end

## CONCEPT GENERATION RULES
Every calendar entry must include a specific concept —
not just a format and platform.

Good concept: "The 60-second squat-proof test
(and why most leggings fail it)"
Bad concept: "Educational post about leggings"

Good concept: "5:47am. Kids asleep. 20 minutes. Go."
Bad concept: "Home workout Reel"

## REPURPOSING FLAGS
For every piece of anchor content (Reels, carousels,
email newsletters), flag repurposing opportunities:
- Which platform it can be adapted for
- What format it becomes
- What changes are needed

## EXECUTION INSTRUCTIONS
1. Confirm calendar type and time period from brief
2. Confirm any campaign themes or product launches
   happening in the period
3. Apply monthly theme structure to weeks
4. Populate day-by-day using weekly rhythm as base
5. Check content ratios per platform
6. Check pillar distribution across the period
7. Flag repurposing opportunities
8. Flag which pieces need video production briefs
9. Save to outputs/FEMFIT_CALENDAR_[PERIOD]_[YYYYMMDD].md

## QUALITY GATES
Before saving output, confirm:
[ ] Content ratios followed per platform
[ ] Every entry has platform, pillar, format, and concept
[ ] Product content does not exceed 30%
[ ] Pillar distribution is roughly even across the period
[ ] Weekly rhythm structure is followed
[ ] At least one repurposing opportunity flagged per week
[ ] Concepts are specific — no vague descriptions
[ ] Video pieces flagged for production briefs
