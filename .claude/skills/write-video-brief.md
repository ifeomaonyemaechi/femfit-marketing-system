# Skill: Write Video Production Brief
# FemFit.fit Marketing Operations System

## WHAT THIS SKILL DOES
Generates a complete video production brief for any
FemFit.fit video content — Instagram Reels, TikTok videos,
or ad creative. Called by the social-media-manager agent.
A production brief tells the person filming exactly what
to shoot, how to shoot it, and what the finished video
should feel like.

## WHEN TO USE THIS SKILL
Use when:
- A content calendar item requires video
- A Reel or TikTok needs more than a caption
- A founder or VA needs clear filming instructions
- An ad creative brief requires video direction

## BRIEF TYPES

### Organic Content Brief (Reels and TikTok)
For: Social media content filmed by founder or team
Detail level: High — covers every shot
Tone guidance: Authentic, real, no overproduction

### Ad Creative Brief
For: Paid advertising video content
Detail level: Very high — includes legal and compliance notes
Tone guidance: Hook-focused, conversion-oriented

### UGC Brief (User Generated Content)
For: Customers or micro-influencers filming content
Detail level: Simple and clear — they are not professionals
Tone guidance: Real, unpolished, genuine

## BRIEF SECTIONS — ALWAYS INCLUDE ALL OF THESE

### 1. VIDEO OVERVIEW (3-4 sentences)
What is this video, why does it work, and what should
the viewer feel at the end. Not the script — the intent.

### 2. PLATFORM AND FORMAT
Platform: Instagram Reels / TikTok / Facebook / Ad
Aspect ratio: 9:16 vertical for all social
Target length: [seconds]
Resolution: 1080x1920 minimum

### 3. HOOK (First 3 seconds)
Visual: Exactly what the viewer sees
Audio: Exactly what is said or heard
Text overlay: On-screen text if any
Why this hook works: One sentence explanation

### 4. SCRIPT OR TALKING POINTS
For talking head: Full word-for-word script
For training footage: Key moments to capture
For list format: Each point as a separate item

### 5. SHOT LIST
Every shot numbered with:
- Shot type (close-up / medium / wide)
- What is in frame
- Any movement (static / pan / follow)
- Approximate duration

### 6. TEXT OVERLAYS
Table format:
| Timing | Text | Position |

### 7. AUDIO DIRECTION
Music style and energy level
Volume relative to speech (e.g. 20% under voice)
Any sound effects or natural audio to keep

### 8. LIGHTING AND ENVIRONMENT
Where to film (home gym / gym / living room / outdoors)
Time of day if relevant
What to avoid (ring lights, blank walls, messy backgrounds)

### 9. WARDROBE
Which FemFit.fit products to wear
Colour guidance (avoid clashing with background)
What not to wear

### 10. PRODUCTION NOTES
3-5 key reminders specific to this video
Common mistakes to avoid for this format

## EXECUTION INSTRUCTIONS
1. Confirm brief type from brief
2. Confirm platform and target length
3. Write all 10 sections in order
4. Be specific — vague briefs produce bad footage
5. Write as if briefing someone who has never filmed before
6. Check against brand voice rules
7. Save to outputs/FEMFIT_VIDEOBRIEF_[CONCEPT]_[YYYYMMDD].md

## QUALITY GATES
Before saving output, confirm:
[ ] All 10 sections present and complete
[ ] Hook section has visual, audio, and text overlay
[ ] Shot list has minimum 5 numbered shots
[ ] Text overlay table has timing column
[ ] Audio direction specifies volume level
[ ] Wardrobe section names specific FemFit.fit products
[ ] Production notes are specific to this video
[ ] No banned brand voice words
