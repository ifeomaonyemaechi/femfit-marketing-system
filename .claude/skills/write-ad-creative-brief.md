# Skill: Write Ad Creative Brief
# FemFit.fit Marketing Operations System

## WHAT THIS SKILL DOES
Generates a complete ad creative brief for FemFit.fit paid
advertising. Called by the ad-copywriter agent. A creative
brief tells the designer or video editor exactly what to
produce — format, copy, visual direction, and audience
targeting. No brief, no good ads.

## WHEN TO USE THIS SKILL
Use when:
- A paid campaign needs creative assets
- A Meta or Google ad needs design direction
- A UGC creator needs a paid content brief
- An existing ad needs a refresh

## AD TYPES COVERED

### Meta Feed Ad (Static Image)
Format: 1080x1080 (square) or 1080x1350 (portrait)
Copy elements: Primary text, headline, description, CTA
Visual: Single product or lifestyle image

### Meta Reels Ad (Video)
Format: 1080x1920 vertical, 15-30 seconds
Copy elements: Hook (on screen), primary text, CTA
Visual: Video with text overlays

### Meta Carousel Ad
Format: 1080x1080 per card, 3-5 cards
Copy elements: Intro card, per-card headline, final CTA
Visual: Product or benefit per card

### Google Search Ad
Format: Text only
Copy elements: 3 headlines, 2 descriptions
Character limits: Headlines 30 chars, descriptions 90 chars

### Google Performance Max
Format: Multiple asset types
Copy elements: 5 headlines, 2 descriptions, 5 image concepts

## BRIEF SECTIONS

### 1. CAMPAIGN OVERVIEW
Campaign name: [name]
Objective: [awareness / traffic / conversions / retargeting]
Budget indication: [if known]
Flight dates: [start and end]

### 2. TARGET AUDIENCE
Segment: [cold / warm / existing customer]
Demographics: Women [age range], interested in [interests]
Behaviours: [what they do online]
Exclusions: [who to exclude]
Platform targeting notes: [any specific targeting]

### 3. KEY MESSAGE
One sentence that captures what this ad communicates.
Not the headline — the core truth the ad must land.
Example: "FemFit.fit gear holds up through real training
sessions, not just photo shoots."

### 4. COPY
Primary text: [full ad copy]
Headline: [text under character limit]
Description: [text under character limit]
CTA button: [Shop Now / Learn More / Get Offer]

### 5. VISUAL DIRECTION
Hero image or video concept: [specific description]
Colour guidance: [palette or avoid list]
Model direction: [real women training, not posed]
Product placement: [how product appears]
What to avoid: [specific visual no-list]

### 6. TEXT OVERLAYS (video ads only)
| Timing | Text | Style |
|--------|------|-------|
| [time] | [text] | [bold/normal] |

### 7. VARIANTS
Provide 2 creative variants per ad:
Variant A: [description of approach]
Variant B: [description of different approach]
Test hypothesis: [what you are testing between A and B]

### 8. SUCCESS METRICS
Primary KPI: [CTR / ROAS / CPA / CPM]
Target: [specific number]
Secondary KPI: [second metric]

## AUDIENCE TARGETING GUIDANCE

### Cold Audience
Women 24-42, interests: fitness apparel, home workouts,
strength training, women's fitness, gym wear
Behaviours: Online shoppers, fitness app users
Exclude: Existing customers, recent website visitors

### Warm Audience
Website visitors (last 30 days)
Instagram and Facebook engagers (last 60 days)
Video viewers (watched 50%+ of organic videos)
Approach: Address hesitation, use social proof

### Existing Customers
Purchased in last 90 days
Approach: Cross-sell to adjacent product category
Exclude: Recently purchased the product being advertised
