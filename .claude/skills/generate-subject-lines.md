# Skill: Generate Subject Lines
# FemFit.fit Marketing Operations System

## WHAT THIS SKILL DOES
Generates 10 subject line variants for any FemFit.fit email.
Each variant uses a different psychological angle. Called by
the email-copywriter agent before finalising any email.

## WHEN TO USE THIS SKILL
Use when:
- Any email needs A/B testing options
- A campaign needs a strong hook before body copy is written
- Existing subject lines are underperforming

## THE 10 ANGLES — ONE VARIANT PER ANGLE

### 1. CURIOSITY
Opens a loop the reader must click to close.
Example: "We almost didn't make these"

### 2. SPECIFICITY
A precise number or detail that signals real information inside.
Example: "48 hours left on your 15% off"

### 3. DIRECTNESS
Says exactly what the email is about. No cleverness.
Example: "Your order has shipped"

### 4. SOCIAL PROOF
References real customers or real results.
Example: "She wore these for 18 months. Still perfect."

### 5. PROBLEM FIRST
Names a frustration the reader recognises immediately.
Example: "Leggings that roll down mid-squat"

### 6. FOUNDER VOICE
Sounds like a person, not a brand.
Example: "Something I built FemFit to fix"

### 7. URGENCY — REAL
Genuine time or stock pressure. Never manufactured.
Example: "Flash sale ends at midnight. No extensions."

### 8. QUESTION
A single question the reader answers yes to immediately.
Example: "Still training?"

### 9. ANTI-HYPE
Deliberately low-key. Stands out in a noisy inbox.
Example: "Just a quiet 15% off"

### 10. BENEFIT LEAD
Leads with what the reader gets, not what you are selling.
Example: "Gear that disappears when you train"

## EXECUTION INSTRUCTIONS
1. Read the email brief or body copy first
2. Generate exactly 10 subject lines — one per angle
3. Keep every subject line under 50 characters
4. Flag the top 3 recommended for testing with a star (★)
5. Explain in one sentence why each starred line was chosen
6. Check all lines against brand voice rules

## QUALITY GATES
Before saving output, confirm:
[ ] Exactly 10 variants generated
[ ] Every line is under 50 characters
[ ] No banned words in any variant
[ ] No exclamation marks unless brief requires urgency
[ ] Top 3 are starred and explained
[ ] No two variants use the same angle
