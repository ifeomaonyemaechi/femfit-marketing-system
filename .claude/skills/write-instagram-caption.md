# Skill: Write Instagram Caption
# FemFit.fit Marketing Operations System

## WHAT THIS SKILL DOES
Generates a complete Instagram caption for any post type —
feed posts, Reels, and carousels. Called by the
social-media-manager agent.

## WHEN TO USE THIS SKILL
Use when:
- Writing a single Instagram caption from a brief
- Batching multiple captions for the week
- A content calendar item needs copy

## CAPTION STRUCTURE — ALWAYS FOLLOW THIS ORDER

### 1. HOOK (Line 1 — visible before "more")
The single most important line. Must stop the scroll.
Rules for hooks:
- Under 10 words
- States a problem, fact, or tension immediately
- No questions that can be answered with "no"
- No "I" as the first word
- No emojis in the hook unless brief specifies

### 2. BODY (Lines 2-6)
Expands on the hook. Tells the story or makes the case.
Rules for body:
- Short paragraphs — 1-2 sentences each
- Line breaks between paragraphs (Instagram formatting)
- No bullet points — write in natural sentences
- Personal and specific, not general and vague

### 3. BRAND TIE (Optional — 1-2 sentences)
Connects the content back to FemFit.fit without hard selling.
Use only when it fits naturally. Skip if it feels forced.

### 4. CTA (Final line)
One question or one instruction. Never both.
Rules for CTA:
- Ask something the audience actually wants to answer
- Or direct them to a specific action (link in bio, tag someone)
- No "double tap if you agree" — too passive

### 5. HASHTAGS (8-12)
Mix of: 2 brand hashtags, 4 niche hashtags, 4 broad hashtags
Always include: #femfit #womensfitness
Never use: #fitspo #motivation (too generic, low signal)

## CONTENT PILLAR MAPPING
Match caption tone to the correct pillar:

REAL TRAINING, REAL RESULTS — Raw, honest, no polish
FIT AND FUNCTION — Educational, specific, helpful
HOME GYM LIFE — Relatable, practical, unglamorous
TRAIN FOR YOU — Philosophical, empowering, never preachy

## EXECUTION INSTRUCTIONS
1. Confirm content pillar from brief
2. Write hook first — test it before writing body
3. Write full caption following structure above
4. Generate 2 CTA variants for testing
5. Check against brand-voice-guardian rules
6. Save to outputs/FEMFIT_CAPTION_[YYYYMMDD].md

## QUALITY GATES
Before saving output, confirm:
[ ] Hook is under 10 words
[ ] No banned words anywhere in caption
[ ] One CTA only (pick the stronger of the 2 variants)
[ ] Hashtags include #femfit and #womensfitness
[ ] Caption sounds like a person, not a brand
[ ] Content maps clearly to one pillar
