# Skill: Write Carousel Script
# FemFit.fit Marketing Operations System

## WHAT THIS SKILL DOES
Generates a complete Instagram carousel script for FemFit.fit
including slide-by-slide copy, visual direction, and caption.
Called by the social-media-manager agent. Carousels are the
highest save-rate format on Instagram — every carousel must
be worth bookmarking.

## WHEN TO USE THIS SKILL
Use when:
- A content calendar item is assigned carousel format
- Educational content needs a swipe-through structure
- A product needs a deep-dive multi-slide treatment
- A how-to or checklist needs visual formatting

## CAROUSEL TYPES

### Educational (Fit and Function pillar)
Purpose: Teach something specific and useful
Slides: 6-8
Hook slide: Bold claim or problem statement
Middle slides: One point per slide, build on each other
Final slide: Summary or save prompt
Best topics: Squat-proof test, sizing guide, care guide,
equipment essentials

### Product Deep Dive
Purpose: Show one product from every angle
Slides: 5-7
Hook slide: The problem this product solves
Middle slides: Feature per slide with specific detail
Final slide: CTA to shop
Best for: New product launches, bestseller spotlights

### Myth Busting
Purpose: Challenge a fitness industry belief
Slides: 5-7
Hook slide: The myth stated plainly
Middle slides: One truth per slide with evidence
Final slide: FemFit.fit philosophy tie-in
Best for: Train For You pillar, brand positioning

### List Format
Purpose: Deliver a ranked or ordered list
Slides: 5-8
Hook slide: What the list covers and why it matters
Middle slides: One item per slide, numbered
Final slide: Save prompt and CTA
Best for: Red flags, essentials, tips

## SLIDE STRUCTURE RULES

### Hook Slide (Slide 1) — Most Important
Must make the viewer swipe immediately.
Rules:
- Bold headline: under 8 words
- Subtext: one sentence that expands the hook
- Visual direction: specific, not generic
- No logo or branding that distracts from the hook

### Middle Slides (Slides 2 to second-to-last)
One point per slide — never two.
Rules:
- Headline: under 6 words
- Body copy: 1-3 sentences maximum
- Visual direction: specific to this slide's point
- Consistent design language across all slides

### Final Slide
Purpose: Convert the swipe into a save or action.
Must include one of:
- "Save this for [specific use case]"
- CTA to shop with specific product mentioned
- Question that drives comments
Never end with just a logo slide.

## DESIGN DIRECTION RULES
Keep direction specific and actionable:
Good: "Close-up of hands stretching fabric over fist —
show pass and fail side by side"
Bad: "Product photo with good lighting"

Colour palette: Deep charcoal and white with one accent
Typography: Bold and legible at small sizes
Photography style: Real and practical, not overly styled

## EXECUTION INSTRUCTIONS
1. Confirm carousel type from brief
2. Plan slide count (5-8 slides)
3. Write hook slide first
4. Write each middle slide in sequence
5. Write final slide with clear save or CTA prompt
6. Write visual direction for each slide
7. Write full caption separately
8. Check against brand voice rules
9. Save to outputs/FEMFIT_CAROUSEL_[CONCEPT]_[YYYYMMDD].md

## QUALITY GATES
Before saving output, confirm:
[ ] Hook slide headline is under 8 words
[ ] Each middle slide has one point only
[ ] Final slide has save prompt or CTA
[ ] Visual direction is specific for every slide
[ ] No banned words in any slide copy
[ ] Caption has hook, body, CTA, and hashtags
[ ] Slide count is between 5 and 8
[ ] Design direction references charcoal and white palette
